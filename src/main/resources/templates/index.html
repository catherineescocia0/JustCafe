Who We Are

More than coffee.
A feeling.

Just Cafe was born in 2020 from a simple idea: a good talk needs a good drink, and a good food makes a better conversation.

We started from a small stall, with only 2 staffs, me and my wife, hand in hand we created a place where people can come in, relax, have a good time with their friends or family or maybe just by themselves; It started with that simple idea, to creating a safe place for everybody.

Just Cafe never forget its origin, we are proud to say that all our coffee beans are locally sourced from Benguet, and Cavite respectively.

Just Cafe is not only a cafe, it is a safe place, a second home, a place to unwind or relax; Good or bad, Just Cafe is always open to welcome you with a hot meal.
